STEPS FOR MAKING POTATO

  1. Cut: Dice potatoes into even 1-inch or 2-inch chunks so they cook at the same rate
  2. Start with Cold Water: Place potatoes in a pot and cover them with at least 1–2 inches of cold water.
  3. Season: Add a generous amount of salt to the water.
  4. Boil: Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer. Cook until "fork-tender" (usually 10–15 minutes), meaning a fork slides in and out with no resistance.
